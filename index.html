FrameImpacts as a Catalysts to achieve positive change in align to Sustainable Development Goals for Development Agencies.

Vision:
Local NGOs/Development Agencies has a strong credibility with transparency and accountability in the socio-economic developmental programs in the North East Region through adapting the M&E system.

Mission:
To co-design effective, inclusive and sustainable model of change to leverage impacts through collaboration and partnership with multiple key stakeholders.

What we do:
The FrameImpacts is a small consultancy firm that seek to help local organisations to adopt and strengthen the M&E system. FrameImpacts believes and promotes partnership and collaboration with consultants and organizations to co-design and strategize the programs/projects and conduct social impact assessments/evaluation. These approaches contributed to a learning and sharing environment amongst the larger team and enabled the co-creation of programmatic solutions. It provides valid and sophisticated insights to socio-economic programmes to enable data-driven decisions.

The FrameImpacts helps to transform reports into visually engaging content to share stories, garner support, and improve the likelihood of comprehension and engagement, in turn building positive social perceptions of organisations. Capacity-building programmes aid teams in conceptualizing programme design, implementing social projects, developing outreach and communication plans, forming strong partnerships, and assessing organisational impact.
